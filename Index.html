Fair Trade

We pay farmers 40% above fair trade prices, ensuring they can invest in their farms and communities. Every purchase directly supports education and healthcare programs in coffee-growing regions.

From Seed to Cup

Our coffee journey begins at high-altitude farms in Ethiopia, Colombia, and Guatemala where farmers use traditional growing methods passed down through generations. We visit each farm twice a year to build relationships and ensure sustainable practices. Every batch is roasted in small quantities to preserve the unique flavors of each origin. The result is a cup of coffee that tastes exceptional while making a positive impact on the planet and the people who grow it.

Direct Trade Relationships

We bypass middlemen to work directly with farming cooperatives. This means more money goes directly to farmers and we can ensure quality at every step. Our partnerships have helped build three schools and two medical clinics in farming communities over the past five years.

Regenerative Farming

Our partner farms use shade-growing techniques that protect biodiversity and improve soil health. Coffee plants grow alongside native trees, creating habitats for birds and insects while naturally enriching the soil. This traditional method produces better coffee while restoring ecosystems.
